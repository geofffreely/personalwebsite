Welcome to my website. I am currently Associate Professor of Philosophy at UC Berkeley. My main research interests are in philosophy of mind, metaphysics, and philosophy of cognitive science and neuroscience. I am currently finishing a book on the philosophy and science of consciousness, tentatively titled “The Search for the Inner Light : Finding Consciousness in a Physical World”.
